SPF99

SPF 99 Radio is the ultimate nostalgic soundtrack for a sunny, carefree afternoon. Dedicated to spinning hits from before the 2000s, this station is a time capsule of feel-good anthems, summer classics, and deep-cut gems that transport listeners to a world before streaming and smartphones. True to its name, SPF 99 Radio isn’t just about music—it’s a call to step outside, soak up the world beyond screens, and embrace the moment. At the heart of its ethos is Baz Luhrmann’s Everybody’s Free (To Wear Sunscreen) speech, a recurring reminder to live fully, protect your skin, and savor life’s simple joys. Whether you’re driving with the windows down, lounging in the park, or just dreaming of golden-hour adventures, SPF 99 Radio keeps the vibe timeless and the energy endless. Put on SPF 99 Radio and soak up the sun.
